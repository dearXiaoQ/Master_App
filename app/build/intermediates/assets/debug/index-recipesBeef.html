SIRLOIN STEAK

Difficulty：medium

Time：30~45 min

Ingredients：500g sirloin steak, 40g butter

Seasonings：10g salt,   10g black pepper，   a lemon,  300g all kinds of vegetables


Preparation

Step 1

Prepare raw materials

500g sirloin steak      40g butter 10g salt      10g black pepper     a lemon 300g all kinds of vegetables


Preparation

Step 2

Treatment of raw materials

In order to avoid retracting , put the steak into the preservation bag and thump, coated the butter in both sides of the steak.


Cooking process

Step 3

Heat the pan and put the sirloin steak in it,fry single side with high heat 3min

Temperature        ℃

Power        W


Start intelligent cooking

Step 4

Turn to another side and sprinkle with some black pepper and salt to fry 3min，Squeeze some lemon juice on the surface（medium）

Temperature        ℃

Power        W


Start intelligent cooking

Step 5

Turn over again and sprinkle with some black pepper and salt to fry. 2min

Temperature        ℃

Power        W


Start intelligent cooking

Step 6

Use oil and juice of the steak when the fried work completed to fry the vegetables2min，now it is ready to serve. Enjoy!
